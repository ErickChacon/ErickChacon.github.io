Dr. Erick A. Chacón Montalván

Senior Research Associate in Environmental Data Science
Lancaster University

I am a senior research associate in environmental data science at Lancaster University. I obtained my PhD degree in Statistics and Epidemiology at Lancaster University and later worked as a part-time lecturer in spatial statistics at Universidad Nacional de Ingeniería. My research focus at Data Science of the Natural Environment (DSNE) is to develop and use spatio-temporal methods to model environmental and health data.
