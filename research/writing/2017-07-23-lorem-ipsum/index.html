A Monte Carlo Likelihood Approximation for Generalized Linear Mixed Models


Andrade, Bernardo B De and Geyer, Charles J

Two new implementations of the EM algorithm are proposed for maximum likelihood fitting of generalized linear mixed models. Both methods use random (independent and identically distributed) sampling to construct Monte Carlo approximations
